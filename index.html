Our Story

Building a cleaner future since 2010

Gyanti Multiservices Pvt. Ltd. is a recognized bio-diesel manufacturer headquartered in Bharuwa Samerpur, Uttar Pradesh. With a robust turnover of ₹116 crore (FY 2024-25), we are committed to delivering alternative fuels and industrial essentials that meet global standards. Our directors Chandra Bhan Singh and Mamta Singh have led the vision to create a greener, job-oriented ecosystem.

We operate under IndiaMART, supplying B100 bio-diesel, DEF (Gyanti Urea), industrial base oil, and N95 masks. Our clientele includes transport fleets, factories, agricultural sectors, and government tenders.
