Science of Sustainability

Our Manufacturing Excellence

Combining advanced chemistry with industrial scale to power a cleaner India.

STEP 01

Feedstock Selection & Preparation

We source high-quality non-edible vegetable oils and used fats. These are pre-heated and filtered in our storage units to remove all moisture and solid particles before the chemical conversion begins.

  • Multi-stage filtration
  • Moisture extraction
STEP 02

The Transesterification Reactor

This is where the magic happens. In our high-pressure reactors, the oil is reacted with methanol and a catalyst. This process breaks down the complex fats into simple methyl esters, commonly known as Bio-Diesel.

Our reactors are automated to ensure 100% conversion efficiency, resulting in a product that burns cleaner than petroleum diesel.

STEP 03

Laboratory Testing & Certification

Every single batch produced at Gyanti Multiservices undergoes 24 strict quality checks in our in-house laboratory. We test for viscosity, density, cetane number, and sulfur content to ensure compliance with ASTM D6751 and EN 14214 standards.
