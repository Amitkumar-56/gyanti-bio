Gyanti Urea (DEF)

High-purity Diesel Exhaust Fluid for modern SCR technology.

Protecting Engines.
Clearing Skies.

Gyanti Urea (DEF) is a high-purity solution consisting of 32.5% technical grade urea and 67.5% deionized water. It is essential for vehicles equipped with Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) systems to reduce harmful Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) emissions, ensuring compliance with BS6 standards while protecting your engine components.
