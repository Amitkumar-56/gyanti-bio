Our Mission & Vision

Pioneering Renewable Energy Solutions

Founded in 2010, Gyanti Multiservices has grown from a local initiative to a multi-million dollar enterprise dedicated to green energy.

A Legacy of Sustainable Innovation

Gyanti Multiservices Pvt. Ltd. was established with a singular vision: to reduce India's dependency on fossil fuels by providing high-quality, renewable bio-energy solutions. Our journey began in Bharuwa Samerpur, Uttar Pradesh, where we recognized the immense potential of bio-diesel in transforming the logistics and agricultural sectors.

Today, with a turnover of ₹116 Crore, we are not just a manufacturer but a technical partner to hundreds of industries. Our B100 Bio-Diesel is produced through a specialized transesterification process that ensures zero sulfur and significantly lower NOx emissions. We specialize in bulk supplies to transport fleets, construction sites, and manufacturing units across Northern India.

Our commitment extends beyond fuel. We have expanded our portfolio to include Gyanti Urea (DEF) to help BS6 vehicles meet environmental standards, and high-grade industrial base oils for heavy-duty machinery. Every product that leaves our facility carries the Gyanti promise of purity and performance.
