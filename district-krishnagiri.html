Krishnagiri: The Mango Capital & Industrial Core

What is Krishnagiri Famous For?

Krishnagiri is known as the 'Mango Capital of India' due to its massive mango cultivation and exports. It is also a significant industrial district in Tamil Nadu, hosting major SIPCOT industrial parks that house some of the world's leading electronics and automobile manufacturing units.

Mango Capital

Major producer of premium mangoes exported to global markets.

SIPCOT Industrial Zone

A thriving hub for electronics, automobile, and precision engineering manufacturing.

Gyanti Plant & Presence in Krishnagiri

In Krishnagiri, Gyanti Multiservices supports the massive manufacturing sector within the SIPCOT zones. We provide high-purity Bio-Diesel and specialized industrial lubricants that are essential for the sophisticated machinery and logistics networks in this industrial heartland.

  • Bio-Fuel for Industrial Manufacturing Units
  • Specialized Base Oils for Electronics Manufacturing
